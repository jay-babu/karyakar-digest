Kids' Diwali Celebration (KDC)
“Life of Shanti” is this year’s Kids' Diwali Celebration (KDC) theme. In celebration of Brahmaswarup Pramukh Swami Maharaj’s Centennial Celebrations, the theme will explain and echo the values taught and demonstrated by Pramukh Swami Maharaj and Param Pujya Mahant Swami Maharaj. Throughout the event, balaks and balikas will explore how Shantilal was an ideal devotee, child, and student via prasangs and rituals. Delegates will learn about the deeply rooted stories in Hinduism and relate them to the prasangs of Bhagwan Swaminarayan and the Gunatit Guru Parampara. ​ Installment 2 is now available on the Karyakar Portal.
