Bal-Balika Syllabus: Unit 2
Unit 2 of the Bal-Balika syllabus will begin on November 13-14. This unit will be about Pramukh Swami Maharaj's life to celebrate his birthday. SKCs for groups 1-3 have been re-branded to the "Shatabdti Series" to celebrate! The first week's content is available on the Karyakar portal. There are 2 weeks that have special presentations:
  • Nov 20-21: instead of a traditional Prasang Varnan for groups 1-3, there will be post-KDC activities to help balaks & balikas retain the KDC messages
  • Dec 18-19: Yog Yagna Sabha
2021 Kids' Diwali Celebrations
The 2021 Kids' Diwali Celebration (KDC) will be broadcasted virtually on Saturday, November 13th, at 10:30 AM during your local time zone. The program will be shown during the Eastern, Central, Mountain, and Pacific Standard Time Zones. Please note that all of the KDC installments are available for download from the portal:
  • Installment 1
    • KDC Theme Memo
  • Installment 2
    • Marketing Material
  • Installment 3
    • Networking Memo
    • KDC Program Activity Prep for Parents
    • Pre/Post-KDC Retention Activity
No additional installments are expected to be released prior to KDC. The post-KDC sabha activity will be conducted in sabha on November 20th/21st.
