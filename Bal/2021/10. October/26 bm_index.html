“In the Joy of Others” Kids’ Diwali Celebration (KDC) 2021
The 2021 Kids' Diwali Celebration (KDC) will be broadcasted virtually on Saturday, November 13th, at 10:30 AM during your local time zone. The program will be shown during the Eastern, Central, Mountain, and Pacific Standard Time Zones. Please note that all of the KDC installments are available for download from the portal:
  • Installment 1
    • KDC Theme Memo
  • Installment 2
    • Marketing Material
  • Installment 3
    • Networking Memo
    • KDC Program Activity Prep for Parents
    • Pre/Post-KDC Retention Activity
  • No additional installments are expected to be released prior to KDC. As noted within the sabha syllabus, the pre-KDC sabha activity should be conducted in Bal Sabha for Groups 1-3 on October 30th/31st. This activity will substitute the Prasang Varnan presentation. The post-KDC sabha activity will be conducted in sabha on November 20th/21st.
Gujarati Class Holidays
Please review the updated Gujarati Class calendar. No classes will be held on November 7th and November 14th weekends in celebration for Diwali and KDC.
