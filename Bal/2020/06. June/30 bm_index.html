Gunbhavi Sampark - Weekly Story Series Launch (WK1)
As mentioned last week, we have developed a plan to reach out to gunbhavi parents during COVID-19. Specifically, by compiling and sharing bal-friendly/generic video clips from past shibirs (e.g., NC18) and our weekly virtual sabhas, we can build goodwill with these gunbhavi families.
There is a five-week plan that consists of the following topics: Importance of Prayer, Strength in Unity, Avoiding Bad Company, Never Giving Up, and Removing Darkness in Our Life. Each of these videos will be sent with a short email that recaps the message from the previous week’s video, and introduces the upcoming video.The Parent Coordinator/Karyakars will drive this effort in each center.
Here you will find the email content and instructions for the first week. Basically, use the suggested subject line, embed the PNG file, and attach the PDF file to the email, then send!

2020 MUKHPATH ASSESSMENT
Mukhpath is essential to satsang growth and is something we place great importance on in sabha. Although we cannot meet in-person, mukhpath can always be practiced at home. For that reason, an at-home mukhpath assessment package is now available.
Mukhpath taught since last year’s exam up to mid-March this year is compiled into a study guide for balaks/balikas to use. Along with the study guide, there are self assessment tools (practice exam, answer sheet, and answer key) included in the package to help balaks/balikas test their knowledge. Parents should actively help their balaks/balikas review and study the mukhpath, then administer the exam. No exams or scores will be collected. Further details can be found in the ReadMe.
