BK Pravrutti Updates – January 2022
Many of you have expressed concern about the Omicron variant and sought guidance for our BK pravrutti accordingly. We wanted to share a few updates for now, and regional teams will share more as they are decided.
Weekly Sabhas, Gujarati Classes & Mandir Darshan
  • Our mandirs will remain open for darshan, and weekly sabhas will still be held where they were currently being held.
  • Gujarati Classes can also continue to be held as they are currently being conducted.
  • Face masks will now be required to be worn by all attendees.
  • We will give out food packets to-go instead of serving food in person.
  • Some centers may decide to conduct virtual sabha, class, etc. as per their regional teams' guidance and communicate it accordingly.
BST/KST Sessions
  • In the same way, BST/KST sessions can still be held in person, or virtually if deemed by the regional team for each center.
  • Since we will not be serving food at our mandirs for the next few weeks, please take that into consideration when deciding whether to hold BST/KST in person or virtually.
  • If the center wishes to hold the sessions in person and serve food, postpone the session to the weekend of January 29-30 and check with the local mandir team at that time if they have started serving food to eat at mandir by then.
  • Face masks will now be required to be worn by all attendees in person.
  • Sleepovers should be canceled for the time being, regardless of vaccination status, until further guidance is provided.
Campus Hangouts
  • Please follow each respective university's guidelines to decide whether to get together in person or virtually.
  • If getting together in person, face masks will now be required to be worn by all attendees, and food should not be served for the time being.
  • Virtual hangouts can be scheduled in place of in person hangouts until university guidelines are updated at each location. Please discuss with Pujya BK Santo and Regional Campus Sabha Leads for further guidance on appropriate formats.
Regional Karyakar Retreats (RKR)
Each region is monitoring their regional situation as it gets closer to their event dates and will announce at least 2 weeks prior to the event.
Yogi Cup
Each region is monitoring their regional situation as it gets closer to their event dates and will announce at least 2 weeks prior to the event.
2022 Bal-Balika Satsang Exams
Study Guides: Bal-Balika Satsang Exam study material is available for download. The study guides are available in English and Gujarati for Satsang Vihar 1-3, and in English for Shishu Satsang Vihar 1. There are also audio books available in English for Satsang Vihar 1-3. The purpose of these study materials is to help prepare balaks and balikas for the exam so that they can confidently sit for the exam. Remember, our 10 in ’22 goal for Satsang Exams is that 90% of registered balaks and balikas sit for the exam.
Online Exam: Due to the ongoing conditions and effects of COVID-19, the Satsang Exam Committee has decided that the upcoming March 2022 Bal-Balika Satsang Exams will be conducted online, similar to last year. A detailed guide and walkthrough explaining the online exam will be provided soon for exam coordinators and parents. The online pre-test exam will be held on 20th February 20th and the final exam will be held on March 13th.
