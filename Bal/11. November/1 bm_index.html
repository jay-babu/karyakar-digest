Akshardham Site Seva Registration Update
As we are all excited to celebrate Pramukh Swami Maharaj's Shatabdi Mahotsav in North America and India, we also have another unique opportunity coming our way to please our gurus. Param Pujya Mahant Swami Maharaj and Pujya Sadguru Santo have been motivating us to come to Akshardham site seva in Robbinsville. Because of their inspiration, we had 300+ kishores & yuvaks come for site seva in the past year. Param Pujya Mahant Swami Maharaj is extremely pleased with the seva everyone has been doing. We now have a chance to continue to do site seva in the following 2 ways:
Winter Break Seva: Since Winter Break is coming up for college students, many have shown interest to come for site seva. Here is KEY INFORMATION to keep in mind while registering everyone:
We need volunteers who can commit a minimum of 15 days of their vacation.
When choosing travel dates for registration, PLEASE make sure you ONLY Arrive/Leave on a Sunday: December 11, 18, 25, + January 1, 8, 15. (This makes it easy for orientation and airport pick up/drop off)
Long-Term Seva: Currently the planning for the Akshardham Mahotsav has started and we are figuring out how many volunteers we will need for each month from January to September in site seva. We will be rolling out registration in phases.
Phase I of registration will be for those volunteers who can commit 6+ months. Assuming that Akshardham would be completed in September, this means these volunteers would need to commit to join site seva from January/February onward.
Phase II of registration will be for those volunteers who would like to commit to less than 6 months. Registration for this phase will launch in January or shortly thereafter, once we review the monthly projections of volunteers required. This will include all of the volunteers who want to come for site seva in the summer months only, etc.
For both types of registration, please connect with your local BKY Vicharan Sant, and they will help you get registered for site seva. You must get permission from your local Pujya Sant before coming for site seva.​
Please do not register for any time period less than 6 months for now, we are only launching Phase I for now.
If you have any questions, please contact your local BKY Vicharan Sant and they can provide clarity.
2023 Satsang Exams
With Diwali and New Year's festivities recently completed, we can all feel the excitement and energy to offer seva and reconnect with our satsang.
There are going to be plenty of opportunities in the coming months to do so - from Pramukh Swami Maharaj's Shatabdi Celebrations in North America and India, and the eagerly awaited North America Akshardham Mahotsav next year.
As many of you already have either come for site seva or are making plans to come for seva, and preparations will be in full swing next year, Pujya Sadguru Santo and senior santo are planning how we can make space in our calendars.
More details will come on other calendar adjustments, but for now, with the guidance of Pujya Sadguru Santo, we will be canceling Prarambh to Pragna satsang exams in March 2023 in North America. Bal exams will still be conducted as planned: 5 total exams ranging from Shishu Satsang Vihar 1 (ages 9-10) to Satsang Vihar 3 (age 13). Registration for the March 2024 Satsang Exams will remain open until September 4, 2023. Those who have already registered for the March 2023 exams will be automatically enrolled for the March 2024 exams.
Everyone is advised to continue their personal satsang reading as is. Pujya Sadguru Santo have seen there is an increase in personal bhakti and reading since the pandemic and encouraged everyone continue that positive trend.
