2020 Virtual Post-Sabha - Goshthi Guidelines

Due to the current limitation on public gatherings, karyakar goshthis will continue virtually for each center. BK Pujya Santo will be attending their centers’ karyakar goshthis based on their availability. Local Coordinators should reach out to their BK Pujya Sant and Regional Coordinator (RC) to schedule a date/time that works for the Pujya Sant and the karyakar team. Local karyakar teams should still continue to take attendance during these goshthis and record the data on BKMS.

2020 Virtual BK Karyakar Goshthi - April

Yoganand: “Yog” means association. We have attained the yog of Bhagwan through the Satpurush. This yog is known as prapti and to experience happiness from this yog is known as praptino kef. Bhagwan Swaminarayan has explained in Vachanamrut Jetalpur 5 that “there is nothing greater than worshipping God.” We have attained Bhagwan himself on this Earth and there is nothing great now to achieve. Any misery we experience now is only a lack of our understanding. How can we tackle any obstacles that stop us from constantly experiencing praptino kef and how can we keep this prapti in mind at all times to truly feel the highest for of happiness?

April Karyakar Goshthi
