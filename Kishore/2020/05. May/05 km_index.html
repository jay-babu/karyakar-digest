2020 BK Virtual Satsang Pravrutti - New Guidelines

All of us are well aware of the changes that the COVID-19 pandemic has brought to our daily lives, especially to our daily Satsang and Satsang Pravrutti. There have been new updates to our Pravrutti and all of you have done excellent in keeping up with the updates and implementing unique processes for your local center.
To help ensure we are on the same page, below is a short summary of new updates or changes to our projects, specifically for May and June. The key updates are highlighted in yellow.

2020 Satsang Exam Results

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, India is under a strict lockdown. Given the unprecedented times, the Satsang Exam Results for March 2020 are delayed without a definite release date. We will announce the new date for the results as soon as it becomes available.
Meanwhile, Param Pujya Mahant Swami Maharaj has provided guidance that all examinees should continue to read Satsang Exam books and begin preparations for upcoming exams. Please inform parents and all examinees.
