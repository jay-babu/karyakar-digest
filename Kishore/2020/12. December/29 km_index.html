Loyā 18

“

Physically a devotee may be blind, disabled, deaf... Does he remain blind or disabled in the abode of God? Certainly not. After leaving these features behind, he assumes a divine form and becomes brahmarup. Therefore, if one should not perceive flaws in the devotees of God, then how can one possibly perceive them in God?



”
