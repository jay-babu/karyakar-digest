2020 Virtual Karyakar Goshthi

Premanand - Happiness from Brahmarup Priti: Bhagwan Swaminarayan’s paramhansas had experienced countless hardships. Yet, when their bhajans and other compositions are analyzed, only messages of happiness are witnessed. Why is this? These paramhansas had completely attached themselves to Bhagwan through love. It was this love that motivated them to do anything required to worship Bhagwan. How can we develop and maintain such love for Bhagwan and the Sant so that we can live our lives aligned to their wishes?

2020 Virtual Summer LAM Notice

Typically, LAM is hosted in April/May to prepare for our summer activities. Without knowing the exact length and severity of the current lockdown, it is difficult to determine the outlook of our activities. Once we have an estimated "opening date" for our Mandirs and Pravrutti, we can determine when to hold and what to discuss at LAM.
Therefore, Summer LAM will be postponed until we can restart our regular activities and meet in person. This meeting would then help all the karyakars reconnect and get on the same page. Additionally, we can use the opportunity to refresh karyakars with our activities and initiatives for the year and help relaunch BK Pravrutti.
In the meantime, we should encourage all of our Bal Coordinators to engage in individual or group conversations with karyakars to check-in and see how they are doing, and provide any resources and information that could be useful to them.
